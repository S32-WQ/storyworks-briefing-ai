AI-Powered Briefing Assistant

This assistant will help in the Discovery Phase, by providing help creating materials such as the brief and the scope of work.

What is it actually?

It's a centralized internal AI-enabled briefing tool that will help to collect information, fill gaps and generate complete, consistent, creative briefs for projects.

How does it work?

The AI analyzes the information from the first meeting with the client. The brief is created by the notes and information gathered from the meeting, the brief from the client, and information from the agency. It gathers the information and creates a SOW according to the project and client.

This tool works like a guided assistant that either side can use to make their workflow smoother, initiate easy communication, and align quickly with the InnovatebyDay theme, supporting government and cultural organizations.

  • Answer guided intake questions
